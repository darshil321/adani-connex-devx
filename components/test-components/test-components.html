Safety

Creating a culture of safety and compliance across every endeavor. Safety is maintained by innovating across planning, implementation, monitoring and optimization of processes related to the protection of people, property and the environment. A formalized system of policies and procedures ensures that employees stay safe while protecting physical assets and the environment.

Environment

We work towards “Zero Harm and Zero Leak” with the belief that all injuries, occupational illness, and environmental pollution are preventable. This commitment is a core part of our operations and is supported by our policies and procedures, driving us to continually improve our OHS&E performance through rigorous monitoring and feedback mechanisms.
